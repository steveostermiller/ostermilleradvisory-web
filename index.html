How I work

We work as a focused, one-to-one partnership. I step in as your integrator, the operating counterpart to a visionary founder. I help you build the internal capability your company needs to run without you firefighting. It follows a simple arc:

  1. 01

    Uncover the frictions

    We surface what’s quietly slowing the company down: the handoffs, bottlenecks, and unclear decisions that keep pulling you back in.

  2. 02

    Simplify operations

    We clarify ownership and decision rights, align the leadership team, and cut the complexity so the right people can move without waiting on you.

  3. 03

    Accelerate growth

    We install a steady execution cadence of short feedback loops, clear priorities, and visible progress so momentum compounds and growth no longer depends on you being everywhere at once.

This isn’t about frameworks for the sake of frameworks. I draw on proven, lightweight ways of working to build habits that actually stick and lead to practical momentum (rather than process for its own sake).

About Steve

I’m Steve — an operator, coach, and author who helps leaders simplify the complexity of growing a business. For more than 25 years I’ve worked at the seam where strategy meets execution: as a COO, a VP of Operations, and a hands-on guide to teams across tech, finance, healthcare, logistics, media, education, defense, federal and state government, utilities, manufacturing, and nonprofit.

I founded and lead Utah Agile, a 2,500-plus member professional community, and I’ve co-authored two best-seller books on modern project delivery. My work is grounded in principles rather than prescriptions, helping companies find what actually works instead of following models that don’t stick.

I’m also a father of five and a soon-to-be grandfather — proof, I like to say, that purpose compounds.
